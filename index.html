Journal Description: Human Sciences and Political Science Welcome to the Journal of Human Sciences and Political Science, a scholarly publication dedicated to exploring the intricate relationship between human sciences and the field of political science. Our journal serves as a platform for researchers, academics, and practitioners from various disciplines to contribute to the advancement of knowledge in these intersecting domains.

We foster an inclusive and multidisciplinary approach, encouraging the submission of high-quality original research articles, theoretical studies, empirical analyses, and methodological innovations. Our aim is to facilitate a deep understanding of the complex dynamics that shape human behavior, societal structures, and political systems.

Topics of interest within the realm of human sciences encompass a broad range of subjects, including psychology, sociology, anthropology, cognitive science, neuroscience, economics, and communication studies. Concurrently, the field of political science encompasses political theory, comparative politics, international relations, public policy, governance, and political economy. We are particularly interested in research that bridges the gap between these disciplines, investigates their interdependencies, and provides valuable insights into how they influence and interact with each other.

Our journal takes pride in maintaining a rigorous peer-review process to ensure the highest academic standards. By engaging leading experts in the field, we guarantee the quality, validity, and significance of the published articles. We also encourage authors to adopt an evidence-based approach, utilizing sound methodologies and providing robust empirical evidence to support their claims.

In addition to original research articles, our journal features insightful reviews, commentaries, and critical analyses that critically evaluate existing literature, challenge prevailing paradigms, and propose innovative frameworks. We aim to foster a vibrant intellectual environment where scholars can engage in constructive debates, exchange ideas, and contribute to the development of new theories and methodologies.

We are committed to the principles of open access, enabling our articles to be freely available to a global audience. This approach ensures the widest dissemination of knowledge and encourages collaboration and engagement among scholars from diverse backgrounds and regions.

We invite researchers, scholars, and practitioners to submit their work to the Journal of Human Sciences and Political Science. Together, let us explore the intricate relationship between human behavior, societal structures, and political systems, and contribute to the advancement of knowledge in these vital areas. Join us in this intellectual journey where the intersection of human sciences and political science holds immense potential to shape our understanding of the complex world we inhabit.
