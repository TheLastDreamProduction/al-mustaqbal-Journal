About Us: Journal of Human Sciences and Political Science The Journal of Human Sciences and Political Science is a scholarly publication dedicated to advancing knowledge and understanding in the intersecting domains of human sciences and political science. Our journal serves as a prominent platform for researchers, academics, and practitioners from diverse disciplines to contribute to the exploration of the intricate relationship between human behavior, societal structures, and political systems. We are committed to fostering an inclusive and multidisciplinary approach, promoting research that bridges the gap between various fields such as psychology, sociology, anthropology, cognitive science, neuroscience, economics, communication studies, political theory, comparative politics, international relations, public policy, governance, and political economy. By encouraging cross-disciplinary dialogue, we strive to generate comprehensive insights into the complex dynamics that shape human societies and their political processes.

Our mission is to facilitate the dissemination of high-quality research that is rigorous, innovative, and impactful. We uphold the highest academic standards by employing a robust peer-review process, ensuring that published articles meet rigorous criteria for originality, validity, and significance. We value the diversity of perspectives and encourage authors to adopt an evidence-based approach, employing sound methodologies and providing robust empirical evidence to support their findings. As an open-access journal, we are dedicated to making knowledge accessible to a global audience. By removing barriers to information, we aim to foster greater collaboration, engagement, and dialogue among scholars, researchers, policymakers, and practitioners worldwide. Our commitment to open access promotes the democratization of knowledge, enabling individuals from all backgrounds and regions to benefit from the latest research findings and contribute to the advancement of the field.
