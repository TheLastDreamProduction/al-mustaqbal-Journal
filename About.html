About Us: Journal of Human Sciences and Political Science The Journal of Human Sciences, Political Science, and Law is a scholarly publication dedicated to advancing knowledge and understanding at the intersection of human sciences, political science, and the field of law. Our journal serves as a prominent platform for researchers, academics, and practitioners from diverse disciplines to contribute to the exploration of the intricate dynamics that shape human behavior, societal structures, political systems, and the legal framework that governs them.

At the core of our journal is a commitment to fostering multidisciplinary scholarship and facilitating meaningful dialogues between the human sciences, political science, and law. We embrace the diverse perspectives and insights that emerge from these interconnected fields, recognizing the significant impact they have on our understanding of the complexities of human society.

Our mission is to publish high-quality original research articles, theoretical studies, empirical analyses, and methodological innovations that push the boundaries of knowledge and contribute to advancements in human sciences, political science, and law. We encourage scholars to adopt an interdisciplinary approach, exploring the intersections and interdependencies between these disciplines to shed light on critical issues, challenges, and opportunities in our societies.

The Journal of Human Sciences, Political Science, and Law upholds the highest academic standards through a rigorous peer-review process. Our Editorial Board comprises distinguished scholars and experts who bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise in their respective fields. They ensure the integrity, quality, and significance of the articles published in the journal, guiding the selection and evaluation process.

We are committed to open access publishing, making our articles freely available to a global audience. This approach fosters equitable access to knowledge, promotes collaboration, and encourages the exchange of ideas across geographic and disciplinary boundaries. By removing barriers to information, we aim to facilitate the dissemination of research findings and encourage meaningful engagement within the academic community and beyond.

The Journal of Human Sciences, Political Science, and Law encourages the submission of not only original research articles but also insightful reviews, commentaries, and critical analyses that contribute to scholarly discourse and challenge existing paradigms. We seek to publish works that bridge the gaps between human sciences, political science, and law, exploring the ways in which these disciplines inform and shape our understanding of human behavior, societal structures, political systems, and legal principles.

We aspire to be at the forefront of academic publishing, embracing technological advancements and innovative practices. Through our online presence, we provide a platform for early access to accepted articles via our Online First section, ensuring the rapid dissemination of cutting-edge research to our readership. Join us in this intellectual journey as we strive to unravel the complexities of human behavior, societal structures, political systems, and the legal landscape. Together, let us contribute to the advancement of knowledge, inform policy debates, and foster a deeper understanding of the interplay between human sciences, political science, and law in shaping our ever-changing world.
